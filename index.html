Welcome to Gol Maldives Resort

Experience the ultimate in luxury and relaxation at Gol Maldives Resort. Located on a secluded island in the heart of the Indian Ocean, our resort offers stunning views of the turquoise waters and white sandy beaches. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway or a family vacation, our resort has everything you need to make your stay unforgettable.

Our Rooms & Suites

At Gol Maldives Resort, we offer a range of luxurious rooms and suites to suit every need and budget. From our cozy beach villas to our spacious overwater bungalows, each accommodation is designed to provide the perfect blend of comfort and style. All of our rooms and suites feature modern amenities such as air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and flat-screen TVs, as well as private balconies or terraces with breathtaking views.

Wine And Dine

Indulge in a culinary journey at Gol Maldives Resort, where our talented chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create a variety of delicious dishes. From international cuisine to local specialties, our restaurants offer a diverse range of dining options to suit every taste. Whether you're in the mood for a romantic dinner for two or a casual meal with friends and family, our resort has the perfect setting for any occasion.

Activities

From snorkeling and scuba diving to sunset cruises and beach volleyball, Gol Maldives Resort offers a wide range of activities to keep you entertained throughout your stay. Explore the vibrant coral reefs, relax on the pristine beaches, or simply soak up the sun by the pool. Our resort also offers a variety of excursions and day trips to nearby islands and attractions, giving you the opportunity to experience the best of the Maldives.
